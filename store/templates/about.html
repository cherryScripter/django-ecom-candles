Welcome To Cozy & Kindled

The Cozy & Kindled Vision

At Cozy & Kindled, we craft soy candles that bring warmth, comfort, and peace to your home. Made from natural ingredients, our candles are designed to create a cozy, eco-conscious ambiance. Each one reflects our passion for quality and sustainability, inviting calm into your space with every flicker.

Our Story

Born from friendship, our journey began as a weekend project and blossomed into a passion for crafting hand-poured candles. We celebrate the beauty of connection and seek mindfulness in every moment. With each eco-friendly candle, we invite you to pause, reflect, and embrace warmth and serenity in your life.
